Life support training

BLS – Basic Life Support

Develop the foundational skills used to recognize life-threatening emergencies, provide high-quality CPR, deliver appropriate ventilations, and use an automated external defibrillator.

Request a BLS CourseCall 713-281-7770

What the course focuses on

Early recognition

Practice identifying when immediate action is needed and activating the appropriate emergency response.

CPR and ventilations

Build confidence with chest compressions, ventilations, and coordinated lifesaving care.

AED use

Learn the role of an AED and how it fits into an organized response to cardiac arrest.

Who may benefit

BLS training is commonly relevant to nursing students, healthcare students, clinical professionals, and others whose school or workplace requires life support preparation.

Confirm the exact course requirement, renewal need, and any prerequisites with your school or employer before registering.
