Pediatric emergency training

PALS – Pediatric Advanced Life Support

Prepare to recognize and respond to respiratory emergencies, shock, and cardiac arrest in infants and children through structured assessment and team-based practice.

Request a PALS CourseCall 713-281-7770

What the course focuses on

Pediatric assessment

Use a systematic process to recognize important changes in an infant or child's condition.

Respiratory and shock response

Build knowledge for responding to pediatric respiratory emergencies and shock scenarios.

Resuscitation teamwork

Practice role clarity, communication, and coordinated response with a healthcare team.

Who may benefit

PALS training is relevant to healthcare professionals whose work may involve emergency assessment or advanced life support for infants and children.

Course prerequisites and employer requirements can differ. Confirm the credential and renewal timing you need before registering.
