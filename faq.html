Course questions, answered

Find practical information about our BLS, ACLS, and PALS training. If your question is specific to an employer or school requirement, contact us before registering.

Courses and eligibility

Which courses does Nightingale RN Academy offer?

We offer three courses: BLS – Basic Life Support, ACLS – Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support, and PALS – Pediatric Advanced Life Support.

Who are the courses for?

Our courses are designed for healthcare students and professionals whose school, employer, clinical placement, or role requires life support training.

How do I know which course I need?

Confirm the exact requirement with your school, employer, or credentialing body. BLS covers foundational life support; ACLS focuses on advanced cardiovascular response; PALS focuses on pediatric emergencies.

Are there prerequisites?

Prerequisites can vary by course and participant. Confirm your requirement first, then contact us so we can verify the current expectations for your requested course.

Scheduling and preparation

How do I request a course date?

Complete our online scheduling form and select BLS, ACLS, or PALS. We will follow up with current availability and registration details.

Can I call instead?

Yes. Call 713-281-7770 if you need help selecting a course or using the scheduling form.

What should I bring?

Required materials can vary. We will provide course-specific preparation and attendance information when your registration is confirmed.

When will I receive confirmation?

Submitting the form is a request for availability. We will contact you with the next steps and confirmation details.

Still have a question?

Tell us which course you are considering, and we will help you understand the next step.
