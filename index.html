Programs

Focused training for exam success, certification readiness, and real-world confidence.

Nightingale RN Academy supports future nurses and healthcare professionals with structured review, practical instruction, and a supportive learning environment. Every course is centered on clarity, preparation, and confidence so students can move forward ready to perform.

Featured Program

NCLEX-RN Review Course

Designed for nursing graduates preparing for licensure, this review course helps students strengthen core concepts, identify weak areas, and approach the NCLEX with a stronger testing strategy. The goal is not only to study more, but to study smarter with clear direction and practical reinforcement.

  • ✓Comprehensive content review across major nursing topics
  • ✓Test-taking strategies that improve confidence under pressure
  • ✓Practice-question focused preparation and guided support
  • ✓Encouraging instruction built around passing the NCLEX
Provider Courses

BLS, ACLS & PALS

These provider courses are ideal for healthcare professionals, students, and clinical team members who need current life support training. Each course is focused on essential response knowledge, procedural confidence, and readiness for professional requirements.

  • ✓Basic Life Support (BLS) for foundational emergency response
  • ✓Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support (ACLS) for advanced cardiac care scenarios
  • ✓Pediatric Advanced Life Support (PALS) for pediatric emergency response preparation
  • ✓Career-ready certification support for healthcare settings

Who these courses are for

Nightingale RN Academy serves aspiring nurses, nursing graduates, healthcare students, and professionals who want focused instruction and a more supportive path to exam or certification readiness.

  • Nursing graduates: Ideal for graduates preparing for the NCLEX-RN and looking for a structured, confidence-building review experience.
  • Healthcare students: Great for students who need BLS, ACLS, or PALS training to support academic and clinical goals.
  • Working professionals: Useful for providers who want to refresh essential emergency response skills and maintain professional readiness.
  • Career-focused learners: A strong choice for those who want serious preparation, practical support, and a polished professional next step.
